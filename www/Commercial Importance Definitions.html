Commercial Importance Definitions

 

Rank: Denotes the rank of a fish species based on this factor.

 

Factor Score: Denotes the scaled initial factor score for each species. Calculated based on the initial factor score standardized to the maximum initial factor score and scaled to range between 10, the maximum score, and 0, the minimum score.

 

Interim Value: Unscaled factor score based on the coastwide revenue by species exponentiated by a value of 0.18 to account for highly variable coastwide revenues by species.

 

Revenue: The summed inflation adjusted ex-vessel revenue across states by species. Source: PacFIN.

 

CA Revenue: Total inflation adjusted ex-vessel revenue associated by within California. Source: PacFIN.

 

OR Revenue: Total inflation adjusted ex-vessel revenue associated by species within Oregon. Source: PacFIN.

 

WA Revenue: Total inflation adjusted ex-vessel revenue associated by species within Washington. Source: PacFIN.

 

Management Group: Management group associated with a species within the fishery management plan.
