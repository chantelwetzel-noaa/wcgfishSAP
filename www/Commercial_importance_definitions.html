Commercial Importance Definitions

 

Rank: Denotes the rank of a fish species based on several factors.

 

Factor Score: Denotes a weighted sum of all factors.

 

Interum [sic] Value: Denotes a value associated with a fish species.

 

Revenue: Total revenue associated with a species along the Pacific Coast states (CA, OR, and WA).

 

CA Revenue: Total revenue associated with a species within California.

 

OR Revenue: Total revenue associated with a species within Oregon.

 

WA Revenue: Total revenue associated with a species within Washington.

 

Management Group: Management group associated with a species within the fishery management plan.
